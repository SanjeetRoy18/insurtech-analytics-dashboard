Xceedance Corporate Health Policy Guidelines:
1. The Standard Health Plan covers up to 80% of in-patient hospitalization costs.
2. Employees are eligible for a maximum Wellness Stipend of $500 per calendar year.
3. Wellness stipends can be used for gym memberships, mental health counseling, and preventative health checkups.
4. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the medical event via the internal HR portal.
